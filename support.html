Common Questions

How do I add a book to my shelf?
Tap the search icon on the Discover tab, search for a book by title or author, then tap it to open and add it to your shelf.
How do I import my Goodreads library?
Go to Settings → Import from Goodreads. Export your Goodreads library as a CSV from the Goodreads website, then select that file in the app.
How do I cancel my premium subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play. On iPhone, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. On Android, open Google Play → Subscriptions.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account and all your data in Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone. Learn more.
I'm having trouble signing in.
Make sure you're using the same sign-in method you used when you created your account (Apple or email). If you're still stuck, email us and we'll sort it out.
