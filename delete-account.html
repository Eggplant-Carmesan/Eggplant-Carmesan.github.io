Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app. This permanently removes your shelf, reading history, stats, and profile.

  1. Open the Bookmark'd app
  2. Tap your profile icon, then tap Settings
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm
Can't access the app?

Email us at wardcarmen91@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and data within 30 days.

Deleting your account removes all data we hold about you, including your bookshelf, reading stats, and account credentials. This action cannot be undone.
